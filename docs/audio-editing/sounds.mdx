title: "Sounds"
icon: "volume"
description: Add sound effects to your project
---

<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="iOS">
            **Automatically add sounds with Magic Edit**

            1. Tap **Sounds**
            2. Tap **Magic Edit**
            3. Choose from the list of options and **Apply**

            **Manually add sounds**

            1. Tap **Sounds**
            2. Tap the **+** icon
            3. Browse the list of sound effects and tap **Apply**

            **Edit sounds**

            - To move the location of a sound, use one finger to drag the soundn to a new location
            - To adjust the volume of a sound, tap **Sounds**
            - To delete a sound, tap **Delete** or **Delete All**
        </Tab>

        </Tab>
