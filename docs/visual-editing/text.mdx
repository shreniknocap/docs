<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="iOS">
            **Add text to your video**

            1. Tap **Text** on the bottom bar
            2. Tap **Add**
            3. Start typing and tap **Apply**

            **Edit the text**

            - Reposition - Use one finger drag it to your desired location
            - Resize - Use two fingers to pinch in or out.
            - Rotate - Use two fingers and rotate
            - Move on the timeline - Use one finger to drag the entire preview earlier or later
            - Adjust the duration - drag either end of the text bar to make it smaller or larger
            - Edit the spelling, font, or color - Tap **Edit**
            - Delete - Tap **Delete** or **Delete All**
        </Tab>

        </Tab>
