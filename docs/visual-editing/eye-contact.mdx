---
title: "Eye Contact"
icon: "eye"
description: "Automatically correct your eye gaze"
---

Applying AI Eye Contact will automatically redirect the eye gaze forward when the subject is reading from a script. For best results, the subject should have their script slightly above or to the side of your camera and remember to blink normally.

<Tip>
For best results, use in combination with the [teleprompter](/docs/create-a-project/teleprompter).

</Tip>

            2. Review the before and after preview
            3. Tap **Apply**
