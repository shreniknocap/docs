---
title: "Eye Contact"
icon: "eye"
description: "Automatically correct your eye gaze"
---

Applying AI Eye Contact will automatically keep your eye gaze forward when reading from a script or teleprompter. Remember to keep the app open and ensure you are connected to a strong internet connection, this process can take a few minutes.

<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="iOS">
            **Apply Eye Contact**

            1. Tap on Eye Contact
            2. Review the before and after preview
            3. Tap **Apply**
            <Tip>
                For best results: Position it slightly above or to the side of your camera for the best results and remember to blink normally and naturally.
            </Tip>
        </Tab>

        <Tab title="Desktop">
            - To add Eye Contact, simply click **Eye Contact** and **Apply**
            - To remove Eye Contact, simply click **Eye Contact** again.
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Android">
            Coming soon to Android. Please sign up for the [waitlist](/docs/start/get-the-app/android).
        </Tab>
    </Tabs>
</div>
