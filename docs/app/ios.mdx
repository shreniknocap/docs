## **Download**
        To download Captions on your iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch you can:
        - [Tap here](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/id1541407007) while on your iOS device
         - Search for "Captions" in the App Store

## Sign Up & Sign In
        To sign up or sign in, select Apple, Google, Microsoft, or your phone number. If you sign up with your phone number, we'll text you a six digit code.

        <Info> We do not support signing up or signing in with an email and password. </Info>
## Availability
        
        - **Devices** - Captions is available on iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch. While the app is available on iPad, please note that it is not currently optimized.
        - **Operating System** - You must be running iOS 15.0 or later to use Captions. [Learn more](https://support.apple.com/guide/iphone/update-ios-iph3e504502).
        - **Languages -** Currently, we offer the app in over [20 languages](/docs/settings/app-language).

        ## **Keeping Captions up to date**

        ****Update to the latest version****

        1. Open the App Store
        2. Tap **Today** (bottom left)
        3. Tap your profile picture (top right)
        4. Pull down to refresh the page
        5. Tap **UPDATE**

        <Tip>Please follow [these instructions](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT202180) to enable automatic updates.</Tip>
