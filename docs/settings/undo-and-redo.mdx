Edit with confidence knowing that you can undo or redo any action while editing.

<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="iOS">
        You can undo and redo as often as you like within an editing session. Once you return to the project screen or close the app, you won't be able to undo or redo any edits.

        <Tip>Before making edits, navigate to the Projects screen, tap on the 3 dots, and duplicate your project.</Tip>
