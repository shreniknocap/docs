On iOS, the Captions app is available in over 20 languages. We are working on translating the app into additional languages on desktop.

<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="iOS">
            **Change the language of Captions:**

            1. Open **Settings** on your device
            2. Tap **Captions**
            3. Tap **Language**
            4. Choose your language
        </Tab>
