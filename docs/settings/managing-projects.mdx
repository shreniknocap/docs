---
title: "Manage Projects"
icon: "rectangle-history"
description: View, manage, and edit your projects
---

<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="iOS">
            In the **Projects** section, you can:

            - View and copy an auto-generated title
            - Generate and copy a description and hashtags
            - Edit your project
            - Duplicate your project
            - Download your project
            - Copy the transcript of your captions
            - Delete your project
            <Warning>
                If you log out, your projects will be deleted.
            </Warning>
        </Tab>

        <Tab title="Desktop">
            In the **Projects** section, you can:

            - View a full list of all projects
            - View the title, dimensions, size, and frame rate
            - Delete your project
            <Warning>If you log out on the web app, all projects will be erased. If you log out on the Mac app, projects will be restored so long as you sign in with the same login credentials.</Warning>
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Android">
            Coming soon to Android. Please sign up for the [waitlist](/docs/app/android).
        </Tab>
    </Tabs>
</div>
