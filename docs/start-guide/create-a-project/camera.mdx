<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
            <Tab title="iOS">
                To record your video in the iOS app:

                1. Tap **Create** > **Camera**
                2. Tap the circle to start recording
                3. Tap the square to stop recording and continue
                4. Choose your [language settings](/docs/captions/add-captions)

                **Tips**

                - You can add a flash, flip the camera, and delete clips
                - You can also add clips from [Captions cloud](/docs/project-settings/captions-cloud)
            </Tab>

            <Tab title="Desktop">
                Captions camera is not available on Desktop.
            </Tab>
            <Tab title="Android">
        Coming soon to Android. Please sign up for the [waitlist](/docs/start-guide/get-the-app/android).
        </Tab>
    </Tabs>
</div>
