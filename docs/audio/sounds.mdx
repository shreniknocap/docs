<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="iOS">
            - To automatically add sounds, tap **Magic Edit**.
            - To manually add sounds, tap the **+** icon and choose a sound effect from the list.

            **Edit sounds**

            - To move the location of a sound, use one finger to drag the soundn to a new location
            - To adjust the volume of a sound, tap **Sounds**
            - To delete a sound, tap **Delete** or **Delete All**
        </Tab>
