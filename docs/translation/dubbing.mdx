---
title: "Dubbing"
icon: "language"
description: "Translate and dub your video into a new language"
---

Dubbing will translate the spoken word and voice match the speaker to match their tone and cadence.


<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="iOS">
            **Dub your video into a new language:**

            1. [Import](/docs/project/import) or [record](/docs/project/camera) your video
            2. Select the language that is being spoken
            3. Select the language that you'd like to translate to
            4. Tap **AI Dubbing** and **Apply**

            **Adjust the volume of your dubbed audio**
            1. Tap on the segment
            2. Tap on **Volume**
            3. Adjust the volume
        </Tab>

        <Tab title="Desktop">
            **Dub your video into a new language:**

            1. [Import](/docs/project/import) your video
            2. Select the language that is being spoken
            3. Select the language that you'd like to translate to
            4. Click **AI Dubbing** and **Apply**
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Android">
            Coming soon to Android. Please sign up for the [waitlist](/docs/app/android).
        </Tab>
    </Tabs>
</div>
