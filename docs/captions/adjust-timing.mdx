---
title: "Adjust Timing"
icon: "bars-progress"
description: Adjust the timing of your captions
---

If the automatic captions don't sync up correctly with the person speaking, you can easily adjust the timing of entire phrases.

<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="iOS">
            **Adjust the timing of captions**

            - Tap on any word or phrase
            - Use 1 finger to drag the word or phrase left or right

            <Note>
                You can also adjust the timing of a specific word [link]
            </Note>
        </Tab>

        <Tab title="Desktop">
            **Adjust the timing of captions**

            - Click **Phrases**
            - Use your pointer to drag phrases left or right
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Android">
            Coming soon to Android. Please sign up for the [waitlist](/docs/start/get-the-app/android).
        </Tab>
    </Tabs>
</div>
