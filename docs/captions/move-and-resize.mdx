---
title: "Move & Resize"
icon: "expand-wide"
description: Change the location, rotation, and size of captions
---


<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="iOS">
            **Adjust captions position and size for the entire project**
            - Location - Use one finger to drag to a new location
            - Rotation - Use two fingers to rotate
            - Size - Use two fingers and pinch in or out to make them smaller or larger

            <Tip>To adjust captions position and size multiple times within a single project, use [Keyframes](/docs/captions/keyframes).</Tip>

        </Tab>

        <Tab title="Desktop">
            - Location - Use your cursor to drag to a new location
            - Rotation - Hover over a corner and drag your cursor left to right.
            - Size - Hover over a corner and drag your cursor diagonally.

        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Android">
            Coming soon to Android. Please sign up for the [waitlist](/docs/app/android).
            
        </Tab>
    </Tabs>
</div>
