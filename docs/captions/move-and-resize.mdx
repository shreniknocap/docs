---
title: "Move & Resize"
icon: "expand-wide"
description: Move, resize, and rotate your captions
---

<Tip>You can also use [Keyframes](/docs/captions/keyframes) to have captions in multiple locations and sizes.</Tip>


<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="iOS">
            - Location - Use one finger to drag to a new location
            - Resize - Use two fingers and pinch in or out to make them smaller or larger
            - Rotate - Use two fingers and rotate
        </Tab>

        <Tab title="Desktop">
            - Location - Use your cursor to drag to a new location
            - Resize - Hover over a corner and drag your cursor
            - Rotate - Hover over a corner to rotate
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Android">
            Coming soon to Android. Please sign up for the [waitlist](/docs/start/get-the-app/android).
        </Tab>
    </Tabs>
</div>
