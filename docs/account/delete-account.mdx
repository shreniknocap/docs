Before you go, please [get in touch](/docs/support) to let us know if there is anything we could have done to improve your experience.

<Warning>Important: Before deleting, please read the information below.</Warning>

- If you have an active subscription, remember to [cancel your subscription](https://help.captions.ai/docs/subscriptions#cancel-a-trial-or-subscription) before deleting your account.
- All projects will be deleted and cannot be restored, please export your projects before you delete your account.
- It is not possible to restore your account, deleting your account is permanent.

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="iOS">
        To delete your account, navigate to your **Profile** > **Delete Account**
    </Tab>

    <Tab title="Desktop">
        To delete your account, navigate to your **[Profile](https://desktop.captions.ai/profile)** > **Delete Account**

    </Tab>
