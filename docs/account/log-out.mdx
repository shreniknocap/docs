How to log out of the Captions app.

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="iOS">
        To log out on iOS, tap **Profile** and **Log Out**.
        <Warning>
            When you log out, your projects will be erased. Please export them before logging out. Once you log back in, you can [recover clips](/project/captions-cloud), but not projects.
        </Warning>
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Desktop">
        **How to log out on desktop**
        To log out on Desktop, click **Profile** and **Log Out**.

        </Warning>
    </Tab>
