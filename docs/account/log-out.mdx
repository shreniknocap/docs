---

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="iOS">
        To log out on iOS, tap **Profile** and **Log Out**.
        
        <Warning>If you log out, your projects will be deleted.</Warning>
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Desktop">    
        To log out on Desktop, click **Profile** and **Log Out**.
        
        <Warning>If you log out on the web app, all projects will be erased. If you log out on the Mac app, projects will be restored so long as you sign in with the same login credentials.</Warning>
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Android">
        Coming soon to Android. Please sign up for the [waitlist](/docs/app/android).
    </Tab>
</Tabs>
