title: "Camera"
icon: "video"
description: Record right in the Captions camera
---

<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
            <Tab title="iOS">
                **Record your video**

                1. Tap **Create** > **Camera**
                2. Tap the circle to start recording
                3. Tap the square to stop recording
                4. Choose your [language settings](/docs/captions/add-captions)

                **Camera Features**

                - You can add a flash and flip the camera
                - You can also remove and add clips from [Captions Cloud](/docs/settings/captions-cloud)
            </Tab>

            <Tab title="Desktop">
                Captions camera is not available on Desktop.
            </Tab>
            <Tab title="Android">
        Coming soon to Android. Please sign up for the [waitlist](/docs/app/android).
        </Tab>
    </Tabs>
</div>
