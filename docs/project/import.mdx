---
title: "Import"
icon: "download"
description: Import your clips into the app
---

<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
            <Tab title="iOS">
                **Import your clips into the iOS app**

                1. Tap **Create** > **Import**.
                2. Browse your camera roll or [Captions Cloud](/docs/settings/captions-cloud) to add clips. After adding each clip, you'll have the option to trim the clip.
                3. Choose your [language settings](/docs/captions/add-captions).

                <Info>On iOS, there is currently a 5 minute limit on all projects.</Info>

            </Tab>

            <Tab title="Desktop">
                To import a clip on desktop, simply click **Upload a Video** or drag your clip into Captions. Currently, we only support importing a single clip, but multiple clips will be supported soon.

                <Info>On desktop, there is currently a 10 minute limit on all projects.</Info>

            </Tab>

            <Tab title="Android">
            Coming soon to Android. Please sign up for the [waitlist](/docs/app/android).
        </Tab>
    </Tabs>
</div>
