title: "Import"

description: Import your clips into the app
---

<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
            <Tab title="iOS">
                Import your clips into the iOS app:

                1. Tap **Create** > **Import**
                2. Choose your first clip to add (If you have more than one clip, tap on the multi-select button)
                3. Trim your video (optional)
                4. Choose your [language settings](/docs/captions/add-captions)
            </Tab>

            <Tab title="Desktop">
                To import a clip on desktop, simply click "Upload a Video” or drag your clip into Captions. Currently, we only support importing a single clip, but multiple clips will be supported soon.
            </Tab>
            <Tab title="Android">
        Coming soon to Android. Please sign up for the [waitlist](/docs/app/android).
        </Tab>
    </Tabs>
</div>
