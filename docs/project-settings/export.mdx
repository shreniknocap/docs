**A few things to know about exports:**

- In order to export, you must be subscribed or in a trial of Captions PRO or Captions MAX [link]. Already subscribed? Restore your [subscription](/docs/start/subscriptions#:~:text=Restore%20your%20subscription%20or%20sign%20in%20on%20a%20new%20device).
- There are no limits to the number of projects you can export.
- Before exporting, you can adjust the [scale](/docs/account-and-settings/scale-and-format) of your project
- To export your project without captions, simply [hide your captions](/docs/captions/hide-and-delete)


<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="iOS">
            **Export your project**

            1. Tap **Export** (on the top right)
            2. Choose your export options
            3. Tap **Export to Camera Roll** (your video will be added to your Photo Album)
            <Tip>
                To export without captions, simply hide your captions before exporting.
            </Tip>
        </Tab>

        <Tab title="Desktop">
            **Export your video**

            1. Click **Export** (on the top right)
            2. Choose your export options
            3. Click **Export Project**
            4. After the video processes, choose your download location on your computer
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Android">
            Coming soon to Android. Please sign up for the [waitlist](/docs/start/get-the-app/android).
        </Tab>
    </Tabs>
</div>
