How to log out of the Captions app.

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="iOS">
        **How to log out on iOS**

        1. Navigate to Profile
        2. Scroll to the bottom

        2. Tap **Log Out**
        <Warning>
            When you log out, your projects will be erased. Please export them before logging out. Once you log back in, you can recover clips, but not projects.
        </Warning>
    </Tab>
    <Tab title="Desktop">
        **How to log out on desktop**

        1. Click on **Profile**
        2. Click **Log Out**
        <Warning>
            When you log out, your projects are still stored locally. If you log back in with the same account details, youâ€™ll be able to access your projects. If you log in with different account details, you can view the project in the project list, but not edit the project.
        </Warning>
    </Tab>
