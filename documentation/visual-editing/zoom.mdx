---
title: "Zoom"
icon: "magnifying-glass"
description: Add zooms to key parts of your video
---

<div id="ios-tab">
        <Tabs>
            <Tab title="iOS">
                **Automatically add zooms with Magic Edit**

                1. Tap **Zoom**
                2. Tap **Magic Edit**
                3. Choose from the list of options and **Apply**

                **Manually add or edit zooms**

                1. Tap **Zoom**
                2. Tap the **+** icon
                3. Choose the intensity
                4. Choose the animation curve and tap **Apply**

                **Edit zooms**

                - To manually add a zoom, tap on the timeline and tap **Add**
                - To edit a zoom, tap on **Edit**
                - To delete a zoom, tap **Delete** or **Delete All**
            </Tab>
