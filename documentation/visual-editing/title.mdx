<Tabs>
    <Tab title="iOS">
        **Add a title**

        1. Tap **Title**
        2. A title will automatically be generated using AI, or you can edit the title manually
        3. Choose from a list of options and **Apply**

        **Remove a title**

        To remove a title, simply tap **Title** and choose **None**
        <Tip>
            When you add a title, any captions that overlap will be hidden. Simply tap on the words and adjust the visibility to unhide them.
        </Tip>
    </Tab>
