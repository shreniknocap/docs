---
title: "Manage Projects"
icon: "rectangle-history"
description: View, manage, and edit your projects
---

<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="iOS">
            In the **Projects** section, you can:

            - View and copy an auto-generated title
            - Generate and copy a description and hashtags
            - Edit your project
            - Duplicate your project
            - Download your project
            - Copy the transcript of your captions
            - Delete your project
            <Warning>
                If you log out, your projects will be deleted.
            </Warning>
        </Tab>

        <Tab title="Desktop">
            In the **Projects** section, you can:

            - View a full list of all projects
            - View the title, dimensions, size, and frame rate
            - Delete your project
            <Note>
                If you log out, projects will be restored so long as you sign in with the same login credentials.
            </Note>
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Android">
            Coming soon to Android. Please sign up for the [waitlist](/documentation/start-guide/get-the-app/android).
        </Tab>
    </Tabs>
</div>
