---
title: "Captions Cloud"
icon: "cloud"
description: Reuse previously used clips
---

Captions Cloud keeps all of your imported clips in one place. This is helpful if you need to log in to another device and reuse your clips.


<div id="ios-tab">
    <Tabs>
        <Tab title="iOS">
            **Recover or reuse clips you imported or recorded in Captions**

            1. In Projects, tap on **Create** > **Import**
            2. Tap on **Recents**
            3. Scroll down and select to **Captions Cloud**
            4. Select your clip(s)

            **Delete clips from Captions Cloud**

            1. Tap **Create**
            2. Tap **Camera**
            3. Tap the image icon (on the top right)
            4. Tap **Delete**
        </Tab>
