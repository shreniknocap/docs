**A few things to know about exports:**

- In order to export, you must be subscribed or in a trial of Captions PRO or Captions MAX [link]. Already subscribed? Restore your [subscription](/documentation/start-guide/subscriptions#:~:text=Restore%20your%20subscription%20or%20sign%20in%20on%20a%20new%20device).
- There are no limits to the number of projects you can export.
- Before exporting, you can adjust the [scale](/documentation/account-and-settings/scale-and-format) of your project
- To export your project without captions, simply [hide your captions](/documentation/captions/hide-and-delete)

<Tabs>
    <Tab title="iOS">
        **Export your project**

        1. Tap **Export** (on the top right)
        2. Choose your export options
        3. Tap **Export to Camera Roll** (your video will be added to your Photo Album)
        <Tip>
            To export without captions, simply hide your captions before exporting.
        </Tip>
    </Tab>

    <Tab title="Desktop">
        **Export your video**

        1. Click **Export** (on the top right)
        2. Choose your export options
        3. Click **Export Project**
        4. After the video processes, choose your download location on your computer
    </Tab>
</Tabs>
